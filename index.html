About Me

I have worked in various fields from being a Public Relation Manager, Executive Secretary to working in an advertising agency. I have lived and worked in various countries. I have an eclectic taste in everything, from books, to art, music and knowledge. I understand the human psych far too well and I am now learning the "psych", if you will, of coding and programming. My imagination is limitless and ongoing and my ability to understand people and behaviour, gives me an upper hand in understanding not only what you require, but also the end user.
